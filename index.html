What is our project about?

SkyGuide AI is an AI-Powered Airline Booking & Travel Assistant.

Main Purpose:
It helps people (especially travelers from Abu Dhabi/UAE) plan their flights and trips more easily and quickly using Artificial Intelligence.

How it helps:
• Chat in natural language
• Suggests flights automatically
• Shows prices & times
• Works in Arabic & English
• Encourages booking via official airlines (Etihad / Emirates)
