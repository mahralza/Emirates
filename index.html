About SkyGuide AI

SkyGuide AI is a smart AI-powered travel assistant developed by Grade 11 students as part of the AI & Technology Project.

It helps travelers from the UAE plan flights, compare options, and get personalized recommendations using advanced prompt engineering and large language models.

Our Mission

To make travel planning easier, faster, and more intelligent for everyone in the UAE.

Key Features

  • Natural conversation in Arabic & English
  • Real-time flight suggestions with price estimates
  • Personalized travel recommendations
  • Clear comparison tables
  • Safety reminders to always check official airline websites

Technology Used

Built using Large Language Models (LLM), Prompt Engineering, HTML, CSS & JavaScript.
