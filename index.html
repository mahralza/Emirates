About Our Project

SkyGuide AI is an AI-Powered Airline Booking & Travel Assistant.

It helps travelers plan their trips easily by chatting in natural language (Arabic & English).

Just type something like:
“Find me a cheap flight from Abu Dhabi to London next month”

What our AI does:

  • ✅ Understands normal conversation
  • ✅ Suggests best flight options
  • ✅ Shows clear comparison tables
  • ✅ Gives personalized travel tips
  • ✅ Always reminds users to book on official websites

This is a Grade 11 AI Project using Prompt Engineering and Large Language Models (LLM).
